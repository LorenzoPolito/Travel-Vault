## It is widely known as ``Hondori''

Hiroshima Hondori Shopping Street is a super wide-area shopping street that represents Hiroshima, the International City of Peace and Culture, and the Chugoku and Shikoku regions. The average number of visitors is about 100,000 per day, and it is a shopping district with more than 200 stores, including retail and service businesses, forming a linear block of about 577m from east to west and a width of 9 to 11m. Located in the center of Hiroshima City, the Astram Line, bus terminal, streetcars, etc. are concentrated in the surrounding area, and it has been developing in coexistence with department stores, supermarkets, large-scale mass retailers, etc. [Contact] 8-18 Hondori, Naka-ku, Hiroshima 730-0035 TEL082-248-1518 FAX082-240-5210
